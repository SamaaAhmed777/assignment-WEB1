My trip to Saudi Arabia

Today was an incredible adventure in Saudi Arabia. The moment I stepped foot in this captivating country, I was greeted by the vast expanse of the desert stretching out before me, with its golden sands shimmering in the sunlight. The weather was warm and dry, typical of the desert climate, which added to the allure of the surroundings.

I began my day by exploring the bustling streets of Riyadh. The towering skyscrapers and modern architecture contrasted with the traditional markets and historic landmarks, creating a fascinating blend of old and new. As I wandered through the maze-like alleyways of Al-Batha souq, filled with the aromas of spices and incense, I couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and wonder.

For lunch, I decided to indulge in the local cuisine at a traditional Saudi restaurant. The flavors of kabsa and mutabbaq tantalized my taste buds, and I savored every bite while immersing myself in the lively atmosphere around me.


kabsa

In the afternoon, I embarked on a desert safari adventure. The thrill of riding over the dunes in a 4x4 vehicle and experiencing the breathtaking views of the desert landscape was truly exhilarating. The vastness and tranquility of the desert left me in awe, reminding me of the beauty of nature's raw power.

As the sun began to set, I found myself mesmerized by the vibrant hues painting the sky as I watched the sunset from the edge of the Empty Quarter. The hues of red, orange, and purple cast a magical glow over the desert, creating a scene straight out of a fairytale.

Now, as I write this entry, I am filled with gratitude for the experiences and memories made today. Saudi Arabia has captured my heart with its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality, and I can't wait to see what tomorrow brings.
